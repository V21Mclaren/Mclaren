Overview

The McLaren Artura marks a bold and exciting new chapter in McLaren’s storied legacy, blending cutting-edge hybrid technology with the brand's signature lightweight design and performance excellence. As the first hybrid in McLaren’s "Core" lineup, the Artura combines a 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 engine with an electric motor to produce a total output of 671 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain enables the Artura to reach 0-60 mph in just 3.0 seconds, delivering an exhilarating experience that combines the best of both worlds—blistering speed and impressive fuel efficiency.

Designed to offer an unparalleled driving experience, the Artura features McLaren’s lightweight carbon fiber MonoCell II chassis, which ensures that the car remains agile and responsive on both the track and the road. This advanced carbon fiber structure contributes to the Artura’s overall weight reduction, allowing for better handling, quicker acceleration, and superior performance without compromising safety or strength. The car's weight-saving measures also include the use of lightweight aluminum, which further enhances its agility and dynamic capabilities.

At the heart of the Artura’s design is McLaren’s relentless pursuit of innovation in aerodynamics and performance. The car features an active rear spoiler that adjusts automatically to enhance downforce at higher speeds, ensuring maximum stability during high-speed cornering and braking. The sophisticated suspension system, featuring a fully independent double-wishbone setup, further optimizes handling and ride quality, offering a driving experience that is both precise and comfortable. Whether negotiating tight corners or tackling straightaways, the Artura remains poised and confident, thanks to its well-balanced chassis and advanced aerodynamics.

And now we get to 2019, the renamed 718 Spyder, and a car that’s basically a more voluptuous, convertible version of the 718 Cayman GT4 (yep, they kept the Cayman bit in its name). So its 4.0-litre flat-six puts out the same 414bhp, the suspension is just as hardcore and identically set up to its coupe sibling, and there’s even a rear diffuser that does proper, actual downforce. The first Boxster to ever provide that.

Inside, the Artura is equipped with a high-tech, driver-focused cockpit designed to enhance both comfort and control. A fully digital instrument cluster provides the driver with real-time performance data, and an intuitive touchscreen interface allows seamless control over key vehicle functions. The cabin is crafted with luxurious materials and offers a bespoke driving environment that is both comfortable and engaging. A key feature of the Artura is its hybrid powertrain, which can run in full electric mode for short distances, offering a quieter and more efficient driving experience when needed.

While the Artura maintains McLaren’s performance-first philosophy, it introduces an environmentally conscious approach to supercar driving. The hybrid powertrain not only delivers extreme performance but also reduces emissions and offers better fuel efficiency compared to traditional supercars. This unique combination of power, efficiency, and cutting-edge technology makes the Artura a pioneer in the evolving landscape of hybrid performance cars.


The Interior

The McLaren Artura’s interior reflects the brand’s commitment to creating a driver-focused, high-performance cockpit that offers a seamless fusion of cutting-edge technology and refined materials. Designed for a truly immersive driving experience, the Artura’s cabin emphasizes comfort, control, and modern sophistication. Its layout is clean and intuitive, with all key controls positioned within easy reach of the driver, ensuring that every action is instinctive and direct.

The interior is dominated by a fully digital display that offers a customizable driver interface, putting vital performance data right at your fingertips. The central touchscreen integrates seamlessly with the digital cluster, offering a wide range of control options for vehicle settings, navigation, and entertainment. The driving experience is enhanced further with a multifunctional sports steering wheel wrapped in high-quality leather and Alcantara, providing a perfect balance of comfort and grip.

The Artura’s seats are designed for optimal support and comfort, featuring McLaren's signature lightweight materials. The standard Sports Seats are wrapped in leather and Alcantara, with high side bolsters to keep you securely in place during aggressive cornering. Optional carbon fiber racing-style seats are available for those seeking an even more performance-focused option. These seats offer a minimalist, race-inspired design and come with advanced adjustment options for the ideal driving posture.

The cabin is finished with the finest materials, including premium leather, Alcantara, and brushed aluminum accents, creating a modern yet luxurious atmosphere. Every detail, from the center console to the door panels, exudes a sense of high-performance craftsmanship. The attention to detail is evident in the stitching, the ergonomic placement of buttons and controls, and the overall fit and finish of the interior.
